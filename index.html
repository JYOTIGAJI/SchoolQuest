Every parent wants to find the right school for their child, but making a meaningful, fact based decision can be difficult. A school open day may present a prettified picture of the school, and it may be hard to make a judgment, as you are led from corridor to classroom, from one freshly cleaned hall to another sanitized study area. League table results can also be misleading, as they may only take into consideration a single exam taken by a year group. How then can you make a fair, and factual evaluation of a school?

SchoolQuest offers up a wealth of information, including such information as average class size, number of teachers, and details of the last Ofsted report.

Perhaps most usefully, SchoolQuest will display the catchment areas, letting you know which schools will be eligible for you to apply to.
